3.4 Result Page: Hit Lists of miRBase and RFam

miREval 2.0 will list all the miRBase miRNAs and RFam ncRNAs displayed in the circle graph at the bottom of the result page.
Users can copy their genomic coordinates or click the hyperlinks to view their infomation pages in these two databases.
