3.2 Result Page: BLAST Result and Visualization

If your sequences are from a speicies recorded in miREval 2.0, miREval will blast your sequences against the genome and generate a circle graph. Blast information is displayed below the circle graph.
The circle graph shows the region of 1000bp flanking your inquiry and provides genomic information within this region, describing by the image below.


Each kind of genomic elements occupies a layer inside the circle with specific color.
Note: On a real result page, you can hover your mouse on these elements to see more information about them.

In addition, miREval will display histograms of phylogenetic shadowing score (the inner histogram) and phylogenetic conservation score (the outer histogram) for your inquiry region, if such infomation for the species is available.
