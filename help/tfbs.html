3.3 Result Page: Transcription Factor Binding Sites

miREval 2.0 will predict potential transcription factor binding sites (TFBSs) regulating your inquiry sequence.
If your inquiry sequence maps to a non-coding region in the genome, TFBSs will be predicted within the region 1000bp flanking your inquiry and displayed in green at "TF_Sites" layer on the circle graph (e.g. the circle graph in last section).
If your inquiry sequence maps to a coding region in the genome, TFBSs will be predicted 1000bp upstream of the transcription start site (TSS) and 500bp downstream. As the circle graph only shows 1000bp flanking your inquiry, TSS might be far away from this region. In this case, the predicted TFBSs outside the circle will be displayed in the list below the graph (See the below example graph).


Note: No matter displayed on the circle graph or not, all predicted TFBSs will be listed with their genomic coordinates under the title "Which transcription factor binding sites potentially regulate to your inquiry sequence?" below the circle graph.
